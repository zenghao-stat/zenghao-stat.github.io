This course builds a measure-theoretic foundation for probability.

Topics include sigma-algebras and probability measures, random variables as measurable functions, Lebesgue integration and expectation, various modes of convergence, and key convergence theorems.

Martingale theory and limit theorems are presented with an emphasis on their role in statistics and stochastic processes.

As a teaching assistant, my work included grading assignments, leading exercise sessions and supplementary tutorials, grading midterm and final exams, and providing regular Q&A support and office hours.
